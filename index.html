WHAT ARE CLASSES?

The iconic Classes of Recon, Support, Assault, and Medic return in Battlefield, each with distinct weapons and gadgets that help you forge a squad that can control any fight. Progress your Class to unlock new traits and customizations as you play.

Career

Pick your Class. Play as the base Combat Role aligned with that class. Unlock more Combat Roles as you play. Switch Combat Roles in a match to adapt to a fight, and dominate the battlefield.

The iconic Classes of Recon, Support, Assault, and Medic return in Battlefield™ V, each with distinct weapons and gadgets to forge a squad that can control any fight. Battlefield V is also delivering more depth to these classes with the introduction of Combat Roles, which bring a package of traits that give players more flexibility during battles.
